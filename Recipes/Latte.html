Delicious Latte

Ingredients

  • Espresso Base:
    • 2 shots of freshly brewed espresso
  • Steamed Milk:
    • 1 cup of whole milk or your preferred milk alternative
  • Frothy Foam:
    • 1/4 cup of milk foam (achieved through frothing)
  • Flavor Enhancers:
    • 1 tablespoon of flavored syrup (vanilla, caramel, etc.)
    • Sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon (optional)
